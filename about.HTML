💜 About MindQuest

MindQuest is a gamified mental wellness website designed especially for teenagers. Instead of treating mental health like a task, MindQuest turns it into a game. Users complete small wellness challenges, track their mood, earn experience points, level up, and unlock badges.

Our goal is to make mental health support more engaging, interactive, and motivating for young people. By combining self-care with game mechanics, MindQuest encourages positive habits such as mindfulness, gratitude, and emotional awareness.

Level up your mind. Build healthy habits. Become the best version of yourself.
